<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle air spring market comprises three main types: convoluted type, sleeve type, and rolling lobe type. Convoluted type air springs have a bellows-like shape and are suitable for heavy loads and rough terrains. Sleeve type air springs consist of a cylindrical rubber sleeve and are commonly used for light to medium loads. Rolling lobe type air springs have an enclosed chamber with a piston-like design, allowing for smooth and comfortable rides. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the commercial vehicle.</p></p>
