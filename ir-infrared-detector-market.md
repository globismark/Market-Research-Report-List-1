Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR) detect a broader range of wavelengths and are commonly used in thermal imaging and defense systems. Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR) have the longest wavelength range and are utilized in applications like temperature monitoring and night vision.
