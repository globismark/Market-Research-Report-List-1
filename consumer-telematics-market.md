<p><p>Consumer telematics market primarily consists of two main types: solutions and services. Solutions refer to the hardware and software components that enable the collection and transmission of data from vehicles to a central server. This includes devices such as GPS trackers, sensors, and onboard diagnostic systems. On the other hand, services encompass the various applications and features that utilize the collected data to provide value-added functionalities to consumers. Examples of services include fleet management, remote vehicle diagnostics, navigation systems, and usage-based insurance. Both solutions and services contribute to enhancing connectivity, safety, and convenience for consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
