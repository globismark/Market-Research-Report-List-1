<p><p>The Solar Water Pump System Market consists of three main types: Surface Pump Systems, Submersible Pump Systems, and Others. Surface Pump Systems are designed to draw water from a source like a well or a lake and pump it to the desired location using solar power. Submersible Pump Systems, on the other hand, are submerged in water and are mainly used in deep wells. The "Others" category includes specialty solar water pump systems that are used for specific applications or in unique environments.</p></p>
