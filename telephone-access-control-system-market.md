<p><p>Telephone Access Control System is a security system that allows or restricts access to a building or premises through the use of a telephone device. It is commonly utilized in apartments, office buildings, gated communities, and other facilities that require controlled entry.</p><p>The system works by connecting a telephone entry system to a central control panel. When a visitor arrives at the entrance, they use the telephone entry system to call a resident or an authorized person. The resident can then grant or deny access by pressing a specific key on their telephone keypad. This allows for secure and convenient access control without the need for physical keys or access cards.</p><p>The Telephone Access Control System Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing need for enhanced security and the rising adoption of advanced access control systems in commercial and residential buildings are driving market growth. The system provides a cost-effective solution to regulate access, minimizing the risk of unauthorized entry.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of telephone access control systems with other security systems such as video surveillance and biometric systems is a growing trend in the market.
