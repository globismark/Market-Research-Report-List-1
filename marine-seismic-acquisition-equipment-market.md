<p><p>The Marine Seismic Acquisition Equipment market can be categorized into three types: 2D Seismic Acquisition, 3D Seismic Acquisition, and Other market segments. 2D Seismic Acquisition involves collecting seismic data along a single line or profile to obtain a two-dimensional image of the subsurface. 3D Seismic Acquisition, on the other hand, captures a more detailed three-dimensional image of the subsurface by collecting seismic data in a grid pattern.
