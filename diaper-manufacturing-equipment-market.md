<p><p>The diaper manufacturing equipment market is divided into two types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic machines are highly automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They can handle the entire diaper production process from core formation to packaging without manual assistance. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines are less automated and require some human intervention at certain stages, such as placing the absorbent core or packaging the diapers.
