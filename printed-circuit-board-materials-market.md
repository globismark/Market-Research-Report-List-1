<p><p>Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Materials market types refer to different components used in the production of PCBs. Photoresist materials are light-sensitive substances applied to PCBs for pattern transfer during manufacturing. Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) is a composite material used as a base for PCBs, primarily consisting of copper foil and resin. Glass Fabric is commonly used as reinforcement material in CCLs, enhancing their mechanical strength. "Others" includes various supplementary components required for PCB production, such as solder masks, dielectric materials, and conductive inks. These materials play crucial roles in the fabrication and performance of PCBs.</p></p>
