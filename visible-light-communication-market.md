<p><p>Visible Light Communication (VLC) is a data communication technology that utilizes light-emitting diodes (LEDs) to transmit information wirelessly through visible light waves. The market is also expected to benefit from the growing need for energy-efficient lighting solutions. These applications require reliable, high-speed, and interference-free communication systems, thus propelling the growth of the VLC market.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in VLC technology, such as the development of Li-Fi (Light Fidelity), have contributed to the market's expansion.
