<p><p>The sauce filling machine market can be classified into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic sauce filling machines are designed to perform all tasks, from filling to capping and labeling, without human intervention. They are more advanced, efficient, and suitable for large-scale production. On the other hand, semi-automatic sauce filling machines require some degree of human involvement, such as placing bottles or containers manually. They are more cost-effective for small to medium-scale productions but may be slower in operation compared to fully automatic machines.</p></p>
