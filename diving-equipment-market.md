Clearance diving refers to diving undertaken for underwater construction, maintenance, or rescue operations, often in hazardous environments. Saturation diving is a specialized method used for deep-sea commercial diving, involving divers staying in a pressurized environment for extended periods. These varying markets cater to different diving needs and require specific equipment to ensure safety and efficiency for the divers.</p></p>
