<p><p>Osteoporosis testing market includes various types of diagnostic tools used to detect the condition. Dexa-Scan, a common method, measures bone mineral density to diagnose and monitor osteoporosis. The FRAX tool assesses the patient's fracture risk based on several factors. CT scans and X-rays provide images of the bone structure, helping to identify fractures. Ultrasounds evaluate bone density and strength by measuring sound waves' transmission through bones. Other methods, such as blood and urine tests, genetic markers, and biomarkers, may also assist in diagnosing osteoporosis.</p></p>
