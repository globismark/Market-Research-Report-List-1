<p><p>The savory flavor blend market consists of various types of flavoring techniques. Masking flavors are used to cover up unwanted tastes in food products. Compounded flavors are created by combining different flavoring agents. Extracts formation involves extracting flavor compounds from natural sources. Emulsification is the process of dispersing oil-based flavors in water-based products. Oleoresins are concentrated flavor extracts derived from spices. Color blending refers to the addition of colorants to enhance the visual appeal of food. Nutraceutical blending involves adding functional ingredients to create health benefits in food products.</p></p>
