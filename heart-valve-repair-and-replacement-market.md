<p><p>Heart valve repair and replacement is a surgical procedure used to treat heart valve diseases. Heart valves play a crucial role in regulating blood flow within the heart. Heart valve repair involves restoring the damaged valve to its normal function, while replacement involves removing the damaged valve and replacing it with a prosthetic valve.</p><p>The heart valve repair and replacement market is witnessing steady growth, primarily driven by the increasing prevalence of heart valve diseases and the rising elderly population. <p><p>Heart valve repair and replacement market includes the Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) and Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair (TMVR) markets. TAVR is a minimally invasive procedure that replaces the damaged aortic valve with a prosthetic valve via a catheter, avoiding open-heart surgery. It is mainly used for patients with severe aortic stenosis. TMVR, on the other hand, repairs the mitral valve using a catheter-based approach. It is employed for patients with mitral regurgitation, allowing them to avoid invasive surgery and achieve improved valve function. Both procedures offer significant benefits in terms of reduced complications and faster recovery.</p></p>
