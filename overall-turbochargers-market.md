<p><p>Overall Turbochargers Market Types - Mono Turbo, Twin Turbo</p><p>The turbochargers market comprises two main types - Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo. Mono Turbo refers to a single turbocharger system fitted in an engine. It increases engine performance and efficiency by compressing air and forcing it into the combustion chamber. On the other hand, Twin Turbo consists of two turbochargers, usually of different sizes, working together to deliver higher levels of power. This configuration optimizes engine performance across a wider RPM range, enhancing overall output. These turbocharger types play a significant role in improving engine efficiency and performance in different automotive applications.</p></p>
