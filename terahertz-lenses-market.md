<p><p>The Terahertz Lenses market consists of various types, including TPX Lenses, HRFZ-Si Lenses, and others. TPX Lenses are made from a thermoplastic polymer with superior optical properties, making them suitable for terahertz applications. HRFZ-Si Lenses, on the other hand, are created using a high-resistivity float-zone silicon material, ensuring low absorption of terahertz waves. These lenses offer exceptional performance in terahertz imaging and spectroscopy. The "other" category includes lenses that are not specifically TPX or HRFZ-Si, but still serve the purpose of focusing and manipulating terahertz waves.</p></p>
