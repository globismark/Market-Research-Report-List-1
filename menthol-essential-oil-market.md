<p><p>Menthol essential oil is available in two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural menthol essential oil is derived from the leaves of the Mentha arvensis plant through a process called steam distillation. It is considered to be pure and free from any artificial additives. On the other hand, synthetic menthol essential oil is produced in laboratories using chemical synthesis methods. While it replicates the aroma and cooling properties of natural menthol, synthetic menthol is not derived from natural sources.</p></p>
