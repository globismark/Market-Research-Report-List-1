<p><p>Resuscitation masks, also known as CPR masks, are medical devices used to provide artificial respiration to individuals in need of emergency breathing assistance. These masks are specifically designed to create an airtight seal over the individual's mouth and nose, allowing for the delivery of oxygen or airflow to the lungs during cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) or other respiratory emergencies.</p><p>The global resuscitation masks market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving this market growth. Firstly, the rising incidences of cardiac arrest and respiratory disorders, coupled with increasing awareness about the importance of early CPR intervention, are propelling the demand for resuscitation masks globally. The growing elderly population, susceptible to cardiac and respiratory complications, also contributes to market expansion.</p><p>In addition, advancements in resuscitation mask technology, such as the inclusion of one-way valves, filters, and transparent materials, are augmenting market growth. These innovations improve the effectiveness and safety of resuscitation procedures. Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has heightened the importance of resuscitation masks by highlighting the need for infection prevention during CPR and other respiratory interventions.</p><p>Geographically, North America and Europe dominate the resuscitation masks market due to well-established healthcare infrastructures, high awareness levels, and favorable reimbursement policies. However, emerging economies in the Asia-Pacific region, such as China and India, are experiencing rapid market growth due to improving healthcare facilities and an increasing focus on emergency medical services.</p><p>Overall, the resuscitation masks market is poised for substantial growth in the coming years. Advancements in technology and increasing awareness about the importance of early CPR intervention are expected to drive the demand for resuscitation masks across the globe.</p></p>
