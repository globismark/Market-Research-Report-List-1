<p><p>Servo presses are advanced industrial machines used in metalworking processes. There are two main types: crank servo presses and screw servo presses. Crank servo presses employ a crank mechanism to convert rotational motion into vertical motion, achieving high force and speed. On the other hand, screw servo presses utilize a screw-nut system to generate force, offering precise control and flexibility in various applications. Both types of servo presses are efficient, reliable, and widely used in manufacturing industries for tasks such as stamping, forming, and punching metal sheets.</p></p>
