<p><p>Decentralized Clinical Trials (DCTs) refer to a modern approach in which clinical trial processes are conducted at the patient's location rather than traditional settings like hospitals or clinics. Market types within DCTs include interventional, observational, and expanded access. Interventional trials involve investigating the effects of a specific treatment on patients, while observational trials focus on observing patients' health outcomes without any intervention. Expanded access trials allow patients to access investigational treatments outside clinical trials when other treatment options have failed or are unavailable, under certain circumstances.</p></p>
