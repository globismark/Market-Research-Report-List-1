<p><p>QC and PD IC (Quick Charge and Power Delivery Integrated Circuit) are technologies used in chargers and power adapters for electronic devices. The market types for these technologies include Downstream Facing Port (DFP), Upstream Facing Port (UFP), and Dual Role Port (DRP). In DFP, the port acts as a power source and provides fast charging to the connected device. In UFP, the port acts as a power sink and can receive power from a charger. DRP, as the name suggests, can perform both functions, acting as a power source or sink depending on the connected device's requirements.</p></p>
