<p><strong>The Network Set Top Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IPTV</li><li>OTT</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Network Set Top Box market consists of three main types: IPTV, OTT, and Other. IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) involves the transmission of television content through internet protocol networks. It enables users to stream media content over IP-based networks, such as LAN, WAN, or the internet. OTT (Over-The-Top) refers to content delivery over the internet, bypassing traditional distribution channels. It allows users to access media content directly from online platforms, without the need for a cable or satellite provider. "Other" refers to any other form of network set top boxes that do not fall under the IPTV or OTT categories.</p></p>
