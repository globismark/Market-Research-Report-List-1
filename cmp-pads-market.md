<p><strong>The CMP Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) pads are essential components used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing surfaces. There are two main types of CMP pads: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are typically made of polyurethane or synthetic materials, providing durability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for polishing hard materials. On the other hand, soft CMP pads are made of porous materials that provide a higher degree of conformability, making them ideal for polishing delicate or sensitive surfaces. Both types serve different purposes in achieving optimal surface uniformity in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
