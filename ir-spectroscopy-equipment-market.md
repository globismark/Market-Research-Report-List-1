<p><p>The IR Spectroscopy Equipment Market is categorized into three types based on technology. Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) Spectrometers utilize an interferometer to measure infrared energy, providing accurate and reliable analysis across a wide range of applications. Near-Infrared (NIR) Spectrometers use light in the near-infrared region to analyze the molecular composition of samples quickly and non-destructively. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various types of IR spectroscopy equipment, such as dispersive infrared spectrometers and time-resolved infrared spectrometers, which are suited for specific applications, such as studying chemical reactions or material properties.</p></p>
