<p><p>Rebar grout sleeve market includes two main types: full-grout sleeves and half-grout sleeves. Full-grout sleeves completely encase the rebar and provide full protection against corrosion. Half-grout sleeves, on the other hand, partially cover the rebar, leaving a portion exposed. This type is commonly used when the rebar needs to be connected to existing structures. Both options offer different advantages depending on the specific construction requirements.</p></p>
