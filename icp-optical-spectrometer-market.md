<p><p>ICP Optical Spectrometer is used for analyzing the elemental composition of materials. It has two market types: sequential type and simultaneous type. The sequential type allows the analysis of one element at a time, sequentially scanning the wavelengths of each element. This type is suitable for applications requiring high accuracy but slower analysis speed. On the other hand, the simultaneous type enables the simultaneous analysis of multiple elements, providing faster results but sacrificing some accuracy. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the analysis.</p></p>
