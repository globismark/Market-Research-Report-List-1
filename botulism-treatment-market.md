<p><p>Botulism is a rare but serious illness caused by the toxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. The primary treatment for botulism involves administering an antitoxin which neutralizes the toxin in the body. This antitoxin is derived from horses and can help minimize the severity of the illness if given promptly. Other treatment approaches may include respiratory support, such as a ventilator, to assist with breathing, and supportive care to manage complications.</p><p>The global botulism treatment market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
