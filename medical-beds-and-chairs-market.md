<p><p>Medical beds and chairs are available in three main types in the market: manual, semi-electric, and electric. Manual beds and chairs require the user to manually adjust the positions using hand cranks or levers. Semi-electric beds and chairs have both manual and electric functions, allowing partial electric adjustments, such as raising or lowering the head or footrest. Electric beds and chairs offer full electric control over various positions, making adjustments effortless with the use of remote controls. These different types cater to different patient needs and provide varying levels of comfort and convenience.</p></p>
