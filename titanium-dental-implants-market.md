<p><strong>The Titanium Dental Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endosteal Implants</li><li>Subperiosteal Implants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium dental implants are used to replace missing teeth and are categorized into different types based on their placement within the jawbone. Endosteal implants are the most common type, where titanium screws are surgically placed directly into the jawbone. Subperiosteal implants are another type, where a metal frame is fitted onto the jawbone and the artificial teeth are attached. The "Others" category includes less common implant types like zygomatic and blade implants. These types provide patients with various options for dental implant procedures depending on their specific needs and jawbone structure.</p></p>
