<p><p>The thermal tape market consists of two major types: single-side and double-side markets. In the single-side market, thermal tapes have adhesive on only one side, making them suitable for bonding components to heat sinks or other surfaces. Double-side thermal tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on both sides, allowing for efficient heat transfer between two surfaces. Both types of thermal tapes are commonly used in electronics, automotive, and other industries where heat management is crucial.</p></p>
