<p><p>The Peripheral Intravenous Catheter (PIVC) market includes different types of catheters. Short PIVC refers to a standard catheter used for short-term purposes. Ported PIVC has an additional access point, allowing multiple infusions or blood sampling. Non-ported PIVC does not have an additional access point. Integrated/closed PIVC combines the benefits of both ported and non-ported catheters, featuring an integrated connector for multiple functions. These variations in PIVC types cater to different medical needs and provide options for healthcare practitioners in administering medication and accessing the patient's bloodstream.</p></p>
