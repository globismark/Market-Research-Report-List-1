<p><ul><li>Parenteral Nutrition</li><li>Enteral Nutrition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clinical nutrition products play a crucial role in providing essential nutrients to individuals who are unable to consume food orally or have specific dietary requirements. Parenteral nutrition involves the intravenous administration of nutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals directly into the bloodstream. On the other hand, enteral nutrition refers to the delivery of liquid nutrition directly into the gastrointestinal tract through a tube, bypassing the mouth. Both parenteral and enteral nutrition markets cater to the diverse nutritional needs of patients to ensure their overall well-being.</p></p>
