<p><ul><li>Protein-based Adhesives</li><li>Collagen-based Tissue Adhesives</li><li>Thrombin-based Adhesives</li><li>Fibrin Adhesives</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile tissue adhesive market can be categorized into different types based on the adhesive composition. Protein-based adhesives are made from natural proteins and are widely used in various surgical procedures. Collagen-based tissue adhesives are derived from collagen, a protein found in animal tissues. Thrombin-based adhesives contain thrombin, a clotting protein, which helps in rapid clot formation. Fibrin adhesives are made from fibrinogen and thrombin mixture and are commonly used in wound closure. Other types of tissue adhesives include synthetic adhesives and cyanoacrylate-based adhesives.</p></p>
