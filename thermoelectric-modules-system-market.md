<p><p>Thermoelectric modules system, also known as thermoelectric coolers or Peltier devices, are solid-state heat pumps that utilize the thermoelectric effect to transfer heat from one side of the device to the other. These modules consist of multiple thermoelectric materials, classified as p-type and n-type, sandwiched between two ceramic plates. When an electric current passes through the module, one side becomes hot while the other remains cool, enabling efficient heating and cooling operations.</p><p>The future outlook of the thermoelectric modules system market is quite promising.
