<p><strong>The Arc Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Arc Lamp</li><li>Short Arc Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arc lamps are categorized into two types - long arc lamps and short arc lamps. Long arc lamps have a longer and wider arc gap, typically ranging between 10mm to 100mm. They are used in applications where a powerful and intense light source is required, such as movie projectors, searchlights, or large outdoor displays. On the other hand, short arc lamps have a smaller arc gap, usually ranging between 1mm to 10mm. They are used in applications where a concentrated and high-intensity light source is needed, for example in medical equipment, microscopy, and stage lighting.</p></p>
