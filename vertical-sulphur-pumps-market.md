<p><ul><li>Multi-stage Pump</li><li>Single-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical sulphur pumps market includes two main types: multi-stage pumps and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers positioned one after another on a common shaft, allowing for increased pressure or head. These pumps are suitable for applications requiring high pressure, such as in oil refineries. On the other hand, single-stage pumps have only one impeller and are used in applications requiring lower pressure, such as in wastewater treatment or drainage systems. Both types are utilized in various industries for pumping sulphur-based fluids effectively.</p></p>
