<p><p>Worm gear drives are mechanical systems used to transmit power between two non-parallel shafts. They consist of a worm, which is a screw-like gear, and a worm wheel, which is a gear-like wheel with teeth. The worm gear drive works by converting the rotary motion of the worm into the linear motion of the worm wheel, resulting in a slower output speed but higher torque.</p><p>The future outlook of the worm gear drives market is expected to be promising. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for automation and robotics in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing. Worm gear drives are widely used in these industries for applications such as conveyor systems, lifting equipment, and rotary tables.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for energy-efficient solutions is also contributing to the growth of the worm gear drives market.
