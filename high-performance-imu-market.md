On the other hand, an accelerometer measures acceleration and is utilized in various devices such as smartphones and vehicles for motion sensing and vibration analysis. Both these sensors play a crucial role in providing accurate and reliable motion-related data, making them essential components in high-performance IMUs.</p></p>
