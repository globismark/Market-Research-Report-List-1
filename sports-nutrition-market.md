Sports protein RTDs are pre-mixed beverages that are convenient for on-the-go consumption. Non-protein products include various supplements like energy drinks and electrolyte beverages. Lastly, sports protein bars are bars fortified with protein, commonly used as a convenient and portable snack for athletes.</p></p>
