<p><p>Implantable Pulse Generators (IPGs) are medical devices used for delivering electrical pulses to specific areas of the body to treat various conditions. Single Lumen IPGs have a single channel or pathway through which electrical pulses are delivered, making them suitable for treating one specific condition. On the other hand, Double Lumen IPGs have two separate channels for delivering multiple sets of electrical pulses simultaneously or sequentially, allowing for the treatment of multiple conditions or different locations within the body. The choice between these IPG types depends on the specific needs and conditions of the patient.</p></p>
