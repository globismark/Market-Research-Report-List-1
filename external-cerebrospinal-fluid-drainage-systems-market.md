<p><ul><li>External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems</li><li>Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The External Cerebrospinal Fluid Drainage Systems market consists of two types: External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems and Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems. EVD systems are used to drain excess cerebrospinal fluid from the brain ventricles, typically after brain surgery or to monitor intracranial pressure. LD systems, on the other hand, drain cerebrospinal fluid from the lumbar region of the spine, commonly for therapeutic purposes or to measure cerebrospinal fluid pressure. Both systems play a critical role in managing conditions related to cerebrospinal fluid drainage and intracranial pressure.</p></p>
