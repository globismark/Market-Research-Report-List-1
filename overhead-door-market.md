<p><p>The overhead door market consists of three main types: sectional doors, rolling doors, and high-speed doors. Sectional doors are made up of horizontal panels that open vertically, offering good thermal insulation. Rolling doors use a curtain-like design that rolls up above the door opening, suitable for applications with limited space. High-speed doors are designed to open and close quickly, enhancing efficiency and reducing energy loss. These market types cater to different needs and industries, ranging from residential garages to commercial warehouses or factories.</p></p>
