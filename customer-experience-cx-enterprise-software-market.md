<p><p>The customer experience (CX) enterprise software market comprises two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed and operated on the company's own servers and infrastructure. It provides organizations with a higher level of control, security, and customization but requires substantial upfront costs and ongoing IT maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is offered as a service over the internet. It eliminates the need for organizations to manage their own infrastructure and allows for scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, but data security and customization may be less under direct control.</p></p>
