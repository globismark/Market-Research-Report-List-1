<p><p>The hydro turbine runner market consists of various types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. The Francis turbine runner is designed for medium-head applications and is the most common type used in hydropower plants. The Kaplan turbine runner is suitable for low-head applications and offers adjustable blades for optimal performance. The Pelton turbine runner is specifically designed for high-head applications and utilizes jet streams to generate power. Other types of runners cater to different specifications and conditions in hydropower plants, providing a diverse range of options for buyers in the market.</p></p>
