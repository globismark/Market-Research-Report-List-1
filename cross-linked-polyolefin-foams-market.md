<p><p>Cross-linked polyolefin foams are versatile materials widely used in various industries for their excellent insulation properties, cushioning capabilities, and lightweight nature. There are three main types of cross-linked polyolefin foams: Polypropylene (PP) Foam, Polyethylene (PE) foams, and Ethyl Vinyl Acetate (EVA) foam. PP foam offers exceptional chemical resistance and thermal insulation, suitable for packaging and automotive applications. PE foams provide superior shock absorption and flexibility, making them ideal for cushioning and protective packaging. EVA foam exhibits excellent durability, flexibility, and resistance to UV radiation, commonly used in sports equipment and footwear manufacturing.</p></p>
