<p><p>The water quality monitor market can be categorized into two types: single parameter monitors and multi-parameter monitors. Single parameter monitors are designed to measure only one specific aspect of water quality, such as pH level or temperature. On the other hand, multi-parameter monitors are capable of measuring multiple factors simultaneously, such as pH, temperature, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen. These monitors offer a comprehensive overview of water quality and are widely used in various applications, including environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and research.</p></p>
