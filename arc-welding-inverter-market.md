<p><strong>The Arc Welding Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MMA</li><li>MIG/MAG</li><li>TIG</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arc welding inverter market can be categorized into different types based on the welding processes they support. These include MMA (Manual Metal Arc) welding, MIG/MAG (Metal Inert Gas/Metal Active Gas) welding, TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) welding, and others. MMA welding uses a consumable electrode to create an electric arc, while MIG/MAG welding utilizes an inert or active gas to shield the welding area. TIG welding utilizes a tungsten electrode to produce the arc. The "others" category encompasses additional welding processes such as plasma arc welding and submerged arc welding.</p></p>
