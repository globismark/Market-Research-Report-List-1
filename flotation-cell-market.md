<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Flotation cell market consists of two main types: cell-to-cell and free-flow flotation cells. In cell-to-cell flotation, the pulp is carried from one cell to another, allowing for an efficient separation process. This type is commonly used in mining operations. On the other hand, free-flow flotation cells allow the suspension of particles to flow without interruption. This design is suitable for larger flotation plants where high capacities and minimal froth carryover are required. Both types contribute to the effective recovery of valuable minerals in various industries.</p></p>
