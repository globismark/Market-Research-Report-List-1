<p><p>The Thermostatic Expansion Valve (TEV) market is segmented into various types, namely solenoid, stainless steel, piezo, and others. Solenoid TEV utilizes electromagnetic forces to control the flow of refrigerant, while stainless steel TEV is made of durable and corrosion-resistant stainless steel material. Piezo TEV operates through the piezoelectric effect, utilizing the electric charge generated by certain materials under mechanical stress. The "others" category encompasses different types of TEV that may have unique features or construction materials, providing flexibility and customization options to suit specific applications.</p></p>
