<p><p>Unified Endpoint Management (UEM) Solutions market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premised. Cloud-Based UEM solutions involve deploying the management platform on a cloud infrastructure, enabling remote access, scalability, and flexibility. It allows organizations to manage various devices and endpoints from a centralized location through the internet. On the other hand, On-Premised UEM solutions entail hosting the management platform locally within an organization's infrastructure, offering greater control over data privacy and security, but limiting accessibility and requiring dedicated hardware and maintenance. Both options offer different advantages, serving the diverse needs of organizations.</p></p>
