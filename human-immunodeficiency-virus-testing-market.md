<p><p>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) testing market includes three main types: Antibody Tests, RNA (viral load) Tests, and Antibody-antigen Tests. Antibody Tests detect HIV antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus. RNA Tests measure the amount of HIV viral genetic material in the blood, known as the viral load. Antibody-antigen Tests simultaneously detect both HIV antibodies and antigens, which are viral proteins. These tests aid in diagnosing HIV infection and monitoring treatment effectiveness, helping healthcare providers offer appropriate care and support to patients.</p></p>
