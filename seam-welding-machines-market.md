Manual Seam Welding Machines: These machines are operated by skilled workers who control the welding process manually. They offer flexibility and precision but are slower compared to automatic machines.</p><p>3. Semi-Automatic Seam Welding Machines: These machines combine the features of both manual and automatic machines. They require some human intervention, offering a balance between speed and control.</p></p>
