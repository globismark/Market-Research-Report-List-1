On the other hand, suspension type harvesters are usually larger and are equipped with suspension systems that allow them to smoothly move over uneven terrain and navigate through crops. These machines provide efficiency and speed in crop harvesting, contributing to the overall productivity of the agricultural industry.</p></p>
