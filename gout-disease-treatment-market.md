<p><p>The Gout Disease Treatment Market consists of different types, namely acute gout and chronic gout. Acute gout refers to sudden and severe inflammatory attacks that usually affect one joint, often the big toe. Patients suffering from acute gout experience intense pain, redness, swelling, and heat in the affected area. On the other hand, chronic gout refers to a long-term condition characterized by recurring gout attacks and the presence of urate crystal deposits in joints and other tissues. It typically develops after repeated episodes of acute gout and may cause joint deformities and chronic pain.</p></p>
