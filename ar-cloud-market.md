<p><p>In the context of AR Cloud, there are two market types: Marker Based and Marker Less. Marker Based AR market relies on pre-determined markers or visual cues in the real world to overlay virtual content, requiring the user to scan and recognize specific markers for augmented reality experiences. On the other hand, Marker Less AR market doesn't rely on markers or visual cues, instead using advanced computer vision technologies to map and understand the real world, allowing virtual content to be placed and interacted with more freely.</p></p>
