<p><p>Zero backlash couplings are used in various industries to connect and transmit torque between shafts. Metal disc couplings use metallic discs to transmit torque, providing high torsional stiffness and accuracy. Composite disc couplings use composite materials, offering lighter weight and excellent vibration dampening. Bellows couplings use thin-walled flexible metal bellows, allowing for angular and axial misalignment. Elastomer jaw couplings use elastomeric material, providing flexibility and damping characteristics. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
