<p><p>The dehiding machine market offers three types of machines: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic vibrators. Electric vibrators are powered by electricity and offer efficient and precise dehiding operations. Hydraulic vibrators use hydraulic power to generate vibrations, providing high force and versatility in dehiding processes. Pneumatic vibrators, on the other hand, rely on compressed air to create vibrations, making them suitable for environments with explosive conditions. Each market type serves specific needs, catering to various industries that require effective and reliable dehiding machines.</p></p>
