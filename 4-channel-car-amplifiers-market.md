<p><p>There are two main types of 4 Channel Car Amplifiers in the market. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) amplifiers are designed and installed by the car manufacturer during the production process. They are specifically tailored to the car's audio system and offer seamless integration. Aftermarket amplifiers, on the other hand, are purchased separately by car owners and installed after the car has been manufactured. They offer more flexibility in terms of customization and are often chosen by individuals who want to improve their car's audio performance.</p></p>
