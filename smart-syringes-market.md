<p><ul><li>Auto Disable Syringes</li><li>Active Safety Syringes</li><li>Passive Safety Syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart syringes market comprises different types of syringes such as auto-disable syringes, active safety syringes, and passive safety syringes. Auto-disable syringes are designed to automatically disable themselves after a single use, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and preventing reuse. Active safety syringes have built-in safety features that are activated during or after the injection to protect against accidental needlestick injuries. Passive safety syringes have safety mechanisms that are automatically activated after use, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries during needle withdrawal and disposal.</p></p>
