<p><p>Overhead traveling cranes, also known as bridge cranes or overhead cranes, are industrial machinery used for lifting and moving heavy objects across a horizontal space within a facility. They consist of a horizontal beam (bridge) supported by two end trucks, which are further mounted on rails on either side of the workspace. The bridge is operated by a trolley or hoist that travels along the length of the beam, allowing the crane to lift and transport objects efficiently.</p><p>The overhead traveling cranes market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Both types provide efficient and flexible material handling solutions, but their specifications and capabilities differ based on the specific requirements of the industry or project.</p></p>
