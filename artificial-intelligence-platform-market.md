<p><p>The Artificial Intelligence Platform market can be classified into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise AI platforms require the installation and deployment of the software on the organization's own servers or data centers. This allows for greater control over data and security but requires extensive IT infrastructure. In contrast, Cloud-based AI platforms are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed remotely through the internet. Overall, the choice depends on factors like data sensitivity, budget, and scalability requirements.</p></p>
