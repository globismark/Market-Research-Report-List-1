<p><p>Pedicure chairs come in two different market types: piped-in type and pipeless type. Piped-in type chairs have a plumbing system that connects to a water supply source, allowing water to flow into the basin and foot spa. This type requires professional installation and regular maintenance. On the other hand, pipeless type chairs have built-in jets and filters, eliminating the need for plumbing connections. These chairs offer convenience, easier installation, and lower maintenance requirements. Both types provide water-based treatments for foot care and relaxation.</p></p>
