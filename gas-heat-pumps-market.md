There are three main types of gas heat pumps: Gas Engine Heat Pumps (GEnHP), Gas Absorption Heat Pumps (GAbHP), and Gas Adsorption Heat Pumps (GAdHP). </p><p>GEnHPs use a natural gas engine to drive a compressor, which then heats or cools the space. GAbHPs use a chemical absorption process to transfer heat, while GAdHPs use a chemical adsorption process. These different types of gas heat pumps offer energy-efficient and environmentally friendly heating and cooling solutions for various applications.</p></p>
