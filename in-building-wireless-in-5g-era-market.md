<p><p>In the 5G era, the in-building wireless market consists of three major types: Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS), Small Cells, and Virtualized Radio Access Network (V-RAN). DAS refers to a network of antennas distributed throughout a building to enhance wireless coverage. Small Cells are low-powered cellular base stations that provide coverage to specific areas within a building. V-RAN is a software-based network architecture that virtualizes baseband functions, enabling efficient use of resources and more flexible deployment of radio access equipment within buildings. These solutions are essential for meeting the increasing demand for high-speed and reliable wireless connectivity indoors.</p></p>
