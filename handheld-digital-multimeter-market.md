<p><p>The handheld digital multimeter market is divided into two main types: 3.5 digit and 4.5 digit. The 3.5 digit multimeters can display three full digits, ranging from 0 to 9, and a half digit that typically shows either a zero or a one. They are generally more affordable and suitable for basic measurement needs. On the other hand, 4.5 digit multimeters can display four full digits from 0 to 9, providing higher accuracy and precision. They are more expensive but preferred for demanding measurement tasks that require greater precision.</p></p>
