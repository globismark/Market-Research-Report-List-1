Spherical hydrophones are designed to detect sound waves in all directions, making them ideal for underwater noise measurements. Miniature hydrophones are small in size and commonly used in medical imaging, underwater robotics, and naval applications. Standard measuring hydrophones are precision instruments that provide accurate measurements of underwater sound sources and are frequently used in scientific research and industrial applications. Each type serves specific purposes within the hydrophone market.</p></p>
