<p><p>The Fingerprint Recognition Sensor Chip Market is categorized into three main types: Optical Sensor, Semiconductor Sensor, and Ultrasonic Sensor. </p><p>1. Optical Sensor: This type of sensor uses light to capture the fingerprint image. It works by illuminating the finger and detecting the reflected light to generate the fingerprint image.</p><p>2. Semiconductor Sensor: These sensors use an array of tiny semiconductor circuits to capture the fingerprint image. The ridges and valleys of the fingerprint alter the electrical current flow, creating a unique pattern that is then converted into digital data.</p><p>3. Ultrasonic Sensor: Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to capture the fingerprint image. They emit ultrasound waves that penetrate the outer layer of the skin and reflect back differently depending on the ridges and valleys of the fingerprint, enabling the creation of a distinctive image.</p><p>These sensor types play a crucial role in the fingerprint recognition technology, enabling secure and accurate identification.</p></p>
