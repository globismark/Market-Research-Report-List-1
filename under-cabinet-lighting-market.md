<p><strong>The Under Cabinet Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incandescent</li><li>Fluorescent</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under cabinet lighting is available in three major types: incandescent, fluorescent, and LED lighting. Incandescent lighting uses a filament that produces light when it heats up, but it is inefficient and has a shorter lifespan. Fluorescent lighting uses a gas-filled tube that emits light when an electric current passes through it, offering higher energy efficiency and longer lifespan. LED lighting employs light-emitting diodes to produce light efficiently, lasting even longer and consuming less energy. LED lighting is considered the most advanced and popular option in the under cabinet lighting market due to its numerous benefits.</p></p>
