<p><p>The Aluminium Casting Products market includes various types of casting methods. Permanent Molding refers to a process where reusable molds are used, resulting in consistent and high-quality products. High Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten aluminium into a steel mold under high pressure, leading to intricate shapes and tight tolerances. Low Pressure Die Casting employs lower pressure to cast larger and more complex parts. Gravity Die Casting relies on gravity to pour molten aluminium into a mold, allowing for cost-effective production of larger components.</p></p>
