<p><p>The Point of Care Pregnancy and Fertility Testing market includes different types of tests for determining pregnancy and assessing fertility. These include the Human chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG) urine test, which is a commonly used pregnancy test that detects the presence of hCG hormone in a woman's urine. The FSH urine test measures the level of follicle-stimulating hormone in a woman's urine to assess ovarian function. The human chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG) blood test is another method of pregnancy testing that requires blood samples. Lastly, the Luteinizing hormone (LH) urine test measures the level of LH in a woman's urine to determine the time of ovulation.</p></p>
