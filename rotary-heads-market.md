<p><p>Rotary heads are devices used in various applications, such as construction, mining, and drilling, to rotate drilling tools or attachments. There are different market types for rotary heads, including hydraulic rotary heads, pneumatic rotary heads, and others. Hydraulic rotary heads utilize fluid pressure to generate rotational motion, offering high torque and stability. Pneumatic rotary heads, on the other hand, use compressed air to create rotation and are known for their lightweight and easy handling. The "others" market category consists of rotary head variants that employ different mechanisms or power sources, such as electric or mechanical drives.</p></p>
