<p><p>The ink and toner cartridges market is divided into two main types: ink cartridges and toner cartridges. Ink cartridges are used in inkjet printers and contain liquid ink that is sprayed onto the paper during printing. Toner cartridges, on the other hand, are used in laser printers and contain powdered toner that is fused onto the paper using heat and pressure. These cartridges are key components for printing and are often sold separately from printers to provide consumers with a choice of replacement options.</p></p>
