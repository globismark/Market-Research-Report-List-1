<p><strong>The ERP Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ERP systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP refers to software that is installed and run on the company's own servers and infrastructure, providing complete control and customization but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based ERP is accessed over the internet and hosted on remote servers, offering more flexibility and scalability while reducing upfront costs. It enables real-time data access from any location, ensuring easy collaborations and updates across departments.</p></p>
