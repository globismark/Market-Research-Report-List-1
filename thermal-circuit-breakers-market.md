PCB mounting thermal circuit breakers are designed to be mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, providing efficient protection for electronic components. Snap-in mounting breakers allow for easy installation and removal in panel cutouts. Panel mounting breakers are designed for mounting on control panels, while plug-in type breakers offer convenience by plugging into sockets.</p></p>
