<p><p>In public market types, an Outdoor Warning System is used to alert people in case of emergencies or dangerous situations. There are three types of warning systems: Omni Directional, Rotating, and Directional. An Omni Directional system emits a loud siren sound in all directions, ensuring that everyone in the vicinity can hear the warning. A Rotating system consists of sirens that rotate, providing a broader coverage area. A Directional system uses speakers or sirens that are strategically placed to emit sound in specific directions, targeting specific areas or populations.</p></p>
