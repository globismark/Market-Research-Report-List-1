<p><ul><li>Air to Water Monobloc</li><li>Air to Water Cylinder</li><li>Air to Water Split</li><li>Exhaust Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CO2 heat pump market types include air to water monobloc, air to water cylinder, air to water split, and exhaust air systems. Air to water monobloc heat pumps are compact units that extract heat from the outside air and transfer it to water for heating. Air to water cylinder heat pumps have an integrated hot water tank. Air to water split heat pumps have separate outdoor and indoor units for flexibility in installation. Exhaust air heat pumps recover heat from ventilation air to provide heating or cooling.</p></p>
