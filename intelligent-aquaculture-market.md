<p><p>Intelligent aquaculture refers to the application of advanced technologies and smart systems in the aquaculture industry. Open system market refers to a type of aquaculture where the farming process takes place in natural bodies of water, allowing the exchange of water with the environment. Closed system market, on the other hand, involves the farming of fish in an isolated and controlled environment, using recirculating systems that filter and recycle water. Both types aim to enhance production efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and ensure sustainable and profitable fish farming.</p></p>
