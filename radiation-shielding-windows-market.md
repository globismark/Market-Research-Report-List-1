<p><p>Radiation Shielding Windows are designed to protect against harmful radiation. Leaded Glass Windows are made by adding lead oxide to glass, which effectively blocks radiation. They provide excellent clarity and durability, making them suitable for medical facilities and laboratories. On the other hand, Leaded Acrylic Windows consist of a transparent plastic infused with lead salts, offering comparable protection to leaded glass but with greater flexibility and lower weight. These windows are commonly used in industrial settings and nuclear power plants where lightweight, shatter-resistant shielding is required.</p></p>
