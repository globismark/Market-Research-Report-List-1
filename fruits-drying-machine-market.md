<p><p>The fruits drying machine market can be divided into two types: Atmospheric Drying Machine and Vacuum Drying Machine. Atmospheric drying machines use warm air circulation to remove moisture from fruits, making them suitable for long-term storage. On the other hand, vacuum drying machines utilize low-pressure environments to evaporate moisture at lower temperatures, preserving the fruits' nutritional value and enhancing their appearance. Both types cater to different needs in the market, providing options for fruit processing and preservation based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
