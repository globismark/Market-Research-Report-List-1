<p><p>Diabetes management devices consist of two main types: blood glucose monitoring devices and insulin delivery systems. Blood glucose monitoring devices are used by individuals with diabetes to measure their blood sugar levels. These devices include glucose meters, continuous glucose monitoring systems, and test strips. Insulin delivery systems, on the other hand, are used to administer insulin to individuals with diabetes. These systems can include insulin pens, insulin pumps, and insulin jet injectors. Both types of devices are crucial in helping individuals manage their diabetes effectively and maintain stable blood sugar levels.</p></p>
