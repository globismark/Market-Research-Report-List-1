<p><strong>The Automatic Hematology Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Part</li><li>5-Part</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Hematology Analyzer market includes two types: 3-Part and 5-Part analyzers. A 3-Part analyzer measures three parameters: red blood cells (RBC), white blood cells (WBC), and platelets. It provides basic information about the patient's blood cell count. On the other hand, a 5-Part analyzer measures five parameters: RBC, WBC, platelets, absolute neutrophil count (ANC), and absolute lymphocyte count (ALC). This type offers a more detailed analysis of the patient's blood, allowing healthcare professionals to diagnose and monitor various blood disorders more accurately.</p></p>
