<p><p>Breast imaging technology has various types, including Molecular Breast Imaging (MBI), Positron Emission Tomography-Computed Tomography (PET-CT), and Positron Emission Mammography (PEM). MBI utilizes a radioactive tracer to detect cancerous cells with higher accuracy, especially in dense breasts. PET-CT combines PET and CT scans to offer both anatomical and functional information about breast tissues. PEM is a molecular imaging technique that visualizes breast tissue at a cellular level. Besides these, there are other emerging technologies assisting in breast imaging, contributing to improved diagnosis and treatment planning in the field of breast cancer detection.</p></p>
