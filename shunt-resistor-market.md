<p><strong>The Shunt Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Resistor</li><li>Through Hole Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shunt resistor market consists of two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) resistor and Through Hole resistor. SMD resistors are small in size and designed to be mounted directly on the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB). They are suitable for high-density applications and offer improved electrical performance. On the other hand, Through Hole resistors have longer leads that need to be inserted through holes on the PCB and soldered on the opposite side. They are preferred for applications requiring higher power handling capability and better mechanical stability.</p></p>
