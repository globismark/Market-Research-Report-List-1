<p><p>Precision farming software is used to optimize agricultural practices by providing real-time data and analysis for decision-making. Local/web-based software is installed on a computer or accessed through a web browser, allowing farmers to manage their operations offline or online. Both types offer benefits in terms of data accuracy, accessibility, and convenience for farmers.</p></p>
