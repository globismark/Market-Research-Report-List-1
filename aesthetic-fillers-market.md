<p><p>Aesthetic fillers are a type of cosmetic treatment used to enhance facial features or restore volume. There are two main types of aesthetic fillers available in the market: absorbable and non-absorbable fillers. Absorbable fillers are made from substances that are gradually broken down and absorbed by the body over time. They typically provide temporary results that can last for several months. On the other hand, non-absorbable fillers are made from materials that do not break down and are considered more permanent. These fillers can provide longer-lasting results, but they may require occasional maintenance treatments.</p></p>
