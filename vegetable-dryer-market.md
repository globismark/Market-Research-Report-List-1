<p><p>Vegetable dryers are categorized into different market types based on their drying methods. Conduction type dryers use direct contact between the vegetables and a heated surface to remove moisture. Convection type dryers use hot air circulation to dry the vegetables evenly. Radiation type dryers utilize infrared radiation to remove moisture from the vegetables. Other types of vegetable dryers may include hybrid models or innovative technologies that combine two or more drying methods. These different market types provide options for manufacturers and buyers based on their specific drying needs and preferences.</p></p>
