<p><p>Veterinary electrosurgery market includes various types of instruments. Bipolar electrosurgical instruments are designed for delicate procedures and feature two electrodes to control the current flow. Monopolar electrosurgical instruments have only one active electrode for tissue cutting or coagulation. Consumables and accessories such as electrodes, footswitches, cables, and pencils are essential components for electrosurgery procedures. They are used for tissue manipulation, coagulation, and cutting. These instruments and accessories play a crucial role in veterinary surgeries, enabling precise and efficient surgical procedures.</p></p>
