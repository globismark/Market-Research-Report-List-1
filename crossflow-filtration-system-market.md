<p><p>Crossflow filtration is a separation process in which a liquid flows through a filter membrane while a portion of it is continuously circulated perpendicular to the filter surface. It is commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and water treatment. Crossflow filtration systems provide high filtration efficiency and low maintenance requirements, making them popular in various applications.</p><p>The future outlook for the crossflow filtration system market is expected to be positive.
