<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The track and field timing system market comprises three types: line-scan system, full-frame system, and break-beam timing system. The line-scan system uses a camera to capture images of the finish line and measures the athletes' positions over time. The full-frame system captures a series of images at high speed, offering precise time measurements. The break-beam timing system utilizes infrared sensors to detect when athletes break the finish line beam. These systems provide accurate timing measurements for track and field events, ensuring fair competition and reliable results.</p></p>
