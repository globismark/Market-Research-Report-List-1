Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a technique that amplifies DNA to identify mycoplasma. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) is an immunological test that detects mycoplasma using specific antibodies. Enzymatic methods involve the use of enzymes to identify mycoplasma contamination. DNA Staining is a technique that uses dyes to visualize mycoplasma in cell cultures.
