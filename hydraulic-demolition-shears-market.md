<p><p>Hydraulic demolition shears are cutting tools used for demolitions in the construction industry. They come in two main types: for excavators and handheld. Hydraulic shears for excavators are attached to the arm of the machine and are operated hydraulically, allowing for efficient and precise cutting. These shears are commonly used for larger demolitions. On the other hand, handheld hydraulic shears are operated manually and are suitable for smaller demolitions where flexibility and maneuverability are required. Both types offer effective cutting power and play a crucial role in the demolition process.</p></p>
