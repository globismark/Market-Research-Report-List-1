<p><p>The Aircraft Energy Storage System market can be categorized into two types: Conventional and Electric. Conventional energy storage systems in aircraft typically rely on fuel-based technologies like hydraulic systems, which store energy through the mechanical modification of fluids. On the other hand, electric energy storage systems utilize advanced batteries, such as lithium-ion batteries, to store and release energy. Electric energy storage systems are gaining popularity due to their ability to provide more efficient and sustainable power solutions for aircraft, reducing dependence on fuel and lowering emissions.</p></p>
