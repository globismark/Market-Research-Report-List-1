<p><p>POV camera market types include CMOS cameras and CCD cameras. CMOS cameras, or complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor cameras, utilize a sensor that converts light into electrical signals. They are known for their low power consumption and compact size. On the other hand, CCD cameras, or charge-coupled device cameras, use a chip to capture and transfer light signals. They offer superior image quality with lower noise levels, but are generally more expensive and consume more power than CMOS cameras. Both types play a crucial role in the POV camera market, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
