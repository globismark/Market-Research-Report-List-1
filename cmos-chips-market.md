<p><ul><li>Active Pixel Type</li><li>Passive Pixel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS chips, also known as Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor chips, are widely used in various electronic devices. In active pixel type CMOS chips, each pixel has its own amplifier, enabling higher speed, low noise, and better image quality. These chips are commonly found in digital cameras and mobile phone cameras. On the other hand, passive pixel type CMOS chips do not have individual amplifiers, resulting in lower image quality and slower operation. These chips are typically found in low-end or older digital cameras and scanners.</p></p>
