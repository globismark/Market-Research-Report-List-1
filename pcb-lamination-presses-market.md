<p><p>The PCB lamination presses market offers two main types of machines: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines are designed to perform the entire lamination process without human intervention, using advanced technology and programming. These machines offer higher efficiency, faster production rates, and reduced labor costs. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human involvement during the lamination process, such as loading/unloading boards or monitoring the operation. While they may not be as efficient as fully automatic machines, they are often more affordable and suitable for smaller-scale operations.</p></p>
