<p><p>Medical grade silicone tubing is commonly used in the healthcare industry for various applications. There are two main types of silicone tubing: platinum vulcanization and peroxide vulcanization. Platinum vulcanization refers to the curing process that uses a platinum catalyst, resulting in high-quality and biocompatible silicone tubing. On the other hand, peroxide vulcanization utilizes a peroxide-based curing agent to achieve similar properties. Both types are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to extreme temperatures and chemicals, making them ideal for medical applications.</p></p>
