Porous soaker hose systems consist of hoses with small holes that allow water to seep out slowly. Emitter drip systems use small tubes with emitters that release water drop by drop. Watermatic drip systems involve placing small emitters at specific intervals along irrigation lines. Micro misting sprinklers create a fine mist that provides water to plants in a gentle manner, ideal for delicate plants or in hot climates.</p></p>
