<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Operating Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Light Source Microscope</li><li>Outside Light Source Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ophthalmic Operating Microscope market consists of two main types: Inside Light Source Microscopes and Outside Light Source Microscopes. Inside Light Source Microscopes have an integrated illumination system, which means that the light source is built into the microscope. It provides a convenient and compact solution for ophthalmic surgeries. On the other hand, Outside Light Source Microscopes require an external light source for illumination. They offer flexibility in choosing different light sources based on the surgeon's preference and can be easily customized according to specific surgical requirements.</p></p>
