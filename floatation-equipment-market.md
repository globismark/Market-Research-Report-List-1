<p><p>Floatation equipment market types can be broadly classified into three categories: pneumatic flotation machines, mechanical agitation flotation machines, and others. Pneumatic flotation machines use compressed air to generate bubbles, facilitating the separation of minerals from the ore. Mechanical agitation flotation machines utilize impellers or rotors to agitate the slurry, promoting the attachment of bubbles to particles. The "others" category includes various types of flotation equipment, such as column flotation machines and flash flotation machines, which are designed to suit specific process requirements and mineral characteristics.</p></p>
