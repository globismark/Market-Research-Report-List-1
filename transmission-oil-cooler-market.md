<p><ul><li>Tube and Fin</li><li>Plate and Fin</li><li>Stacked Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission oil cooler market comprises of three major types: tube and fin, plate and fin, and stacked plate. Tube and fin coolers consist of a series of tubes surrounded by fins for increased surface area and heat dissipation. Plate and fin coolers utilize flat plates with small channels for oil flow, offering higher efficiency in cooling. Stacked plate coolers are similar to plate and fin coolers but consist of stacked plates, providing enhanced cooling capacity. These variations cater to different customer preferences and cooling requirements in the transmission oil cooler market.</p></p>
