<p><p>Steam compressors are used in various industries to compress steam for different applications. There are two main types of steam compressors in the market: Roots-Type and Centrifugal. </p><p>Roots-Type compressors are positive displacement machines that use two meshing rotors to compress steam. They are efficient at handling low to medium pressure steam and are commonly used in applications where a constant flow is required, such as in power plants.</p><p>On the other hand, Centrifugal compressors use high-speed rotating impellers to accelerate the steam, converting its kinetic energy into pressure energy. They are widely used in applications requiring high pressure levels, such as in oil refineries and chemical plants.</p><p>Both types have their own advantages and limitations, and the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of each application.</p></p>
