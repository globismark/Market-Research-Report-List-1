<p><p>Surface temperature sensors are devices used to measure the temperature of objects by coming into direct contact with their surface. The market for these sensors can be categorized into different types based on their technology. Thermocouples use the principle of thermoelectricity to measure temperature, while RTDs (Resistance Temperature Detectors) rely on the change in electrical resistance of a metal wire with temperature. Infrared sensors, on the other hand, detect thermal radiation emitted by the object. Additionally, there are other types of surface temperature sensors available in the market that employ various technologies to measure temperature accurately. These sensors find application in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
