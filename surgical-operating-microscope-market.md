<p><p>The Surgical Operating Microscope market consists of two types: electronic and optical. Electronic microscopes utilize digital imaging technology, allowing surgeons to view high-resolution images on a screen. They offer zoom and image enhancement features for accurate viewing during complex surgical procedures. Optical microscopes, on the other hand, use optical lenses and illumination systems to provide surgeons with a magnified and illuminated view of the operating area. Both types of microscopes play a vital role in surgical procedures by improving visualization and precision for surgeons.</p></p>
