<p><p>The automotive hemming adhesive market can be categorized into one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use and do not require mixing, making them more convenient and easier to apply. Two-component adhesives, on the other hand, require mixing of two components before application, providing a stronger and more durable bond. One-component adhesives are preferred for quick and easy applications, while two-component adhesives are favored for their superior strength and performance in more demanding automotive hemming applications.</p></p>
