<p><p>The HVAC system analyzer market can be broadly categorized into two types: analog and digital. Analog HVAC system analyzers comprise traditional devices that use analog signals to measure and display values such as temperature, pressure, and air quality. These analyzers are simple to use and do not require complex software. In contrast, digital HVAC system analyzers leverage advanced technology to provide precise measurements and data analysis. They often feature digital displays, memory storage, and connectivity options, allowing for more accurate and efficient monitoring and diagnostics of HVAC systems.</p></p>
