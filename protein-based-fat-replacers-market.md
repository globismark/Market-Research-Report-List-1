<p><p>Protein-based fat replacers are a type of food ingredient used to lower the fat content in various food products. These fat replacers come in different market types such as microparticulated protein, modified whey protein concentrate, and others. Microparticulated protein refers to proteins that have been broken down into small particles to mimic the texture and mouthfeel of fat. Modified whey protein concentrate involves altering the whey protein to enhance its emulsifying properties. Other types of protein-based fat replacers may include soy protein, pea protein, or egg protein, which can also provide functional and nutritional benefits in food products.</p></p>
