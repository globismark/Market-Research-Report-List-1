<p><p>The Image Sensor Test System market consists of three major types: CMOS, CCD, and CIS. CMOS stands for complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor and is popular due to its low power consumption, faster readout speed, and integration of image processing features. CCD, or charge-coupled device, is known for its high-quality image output, low noise, and suitability for low-light conditions. CIS, or contact image sensor, offers a compact and lightweight design, making it suitable for portable devices. These different types cater to diverse needs, providing a range of options for image sensor testing.</p></p>
