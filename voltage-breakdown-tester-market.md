<p><p>A Voltage Breakdown Tester, also known as a Dielectric Strength Tester, is a device used to measure the voltage at which an electrical insulator, such as a cable or transformer, breaks down and allows current to flow. It is widely used across various industries to ensure the safety and reliability of electrical equipment.</p><p>The Voltage Breakdown Tester directly applies a high voltage across the insulator and measures the current flowing through it. By gradually increasing the voltage, the tester determines the point at which the insulator fails, indicating its breakdown voltage or dielectric strength. This information is crucial for quality control and preventive maintenance of electrical systems.</p><p>The Voltage Breakdown Tester Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
