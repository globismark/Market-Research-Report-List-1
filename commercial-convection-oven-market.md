<p><p>Commercial convection ovens are available in two main types: countertop and floor model. Countertop convection ovens are compact and designed to sit on a countertop, making them ideal for smaller commercial kitchens or establishments with limited space. On the other hand, floor model convection ovens are larger and freestanding, designed to be placed on the floor. These ovens are commonly found in larger commercial kitchens and are capable of handling larger cooking volumes. Both types of ovens offer convection cooking methods, which use a fan to circulate hot air for faster and more even cooking results.</p></p>
