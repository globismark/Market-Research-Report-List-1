<p><ul><li>Channel Type</li><li>Clip Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray film hanger market can be divided into two types: channel type and clip type. In the channel type market, the film hangers are designed with open-ended slots or channels that hold the X-ray films securely. These hangers allow for easy insertion and removal of the films. On the other hand, the clip type market involves film hangers equipped with clips that securely hold the X-ray films in place. These clips ensure that the films do not slide or move during the imaging process, providing accurate and reliable results.</p></p>
