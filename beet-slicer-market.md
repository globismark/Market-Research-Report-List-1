<p><p>The Beet Slicer market encompasses two main types: the Drum Beet Slicer and the Disc Beet Slicer. The Drum Beet Slicer consists of a rotating drum with blades that slice the beets into thin pieces as they pass through. This type is commonly used in large-scale industrial settings due to its high efficiency and capacity. On the other hand, the Disc Beet Slicer features sharp discs that cut the beets into uniform slices. This type is more suitable for smaller-scale operations, such as home kitchens or small food processing facilities.</p></p>
