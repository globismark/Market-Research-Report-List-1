<p><p>The spine navigation market consists of different types of technologies used for surgical navigation during spine procedures. Electromagnetic spinal navigation relies on electromagnetic tracking systems to guide surgeons and provide real-time imaging. Optical spinal navigation employs optical tracking technology using cameras and specialized markers for accurate positioning. Other types of spinal navigation include probe-based navigation, robotic-assisted navigation, and computer-assisted navigation. These technologies enhance surgical precision, improve patient outcomes, and enable minimally invasive procedures in spinal surgeries.</p></p>
