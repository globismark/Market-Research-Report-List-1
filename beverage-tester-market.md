<p><p>The beverage tester market can be categorized into two types: handheld and benchtop testers. Handheld beverage testers are portable devices that allow users to quickly and conveniently test the quality and composition of beverages on the go. They are widely used by bars, restaurants, and beverage manufacturers. On the other hand, benchtop beverage testers are larger and more sophisticated instruments that are typically used in laboratory settings for comprehensive analysis of beverages. They provide accurate and detailed measurements of various parameters such as pH levels, alcohol content, and sugar concentration.</p></p>
