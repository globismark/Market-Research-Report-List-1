Progesterone Therapy involves the administration of progesterone hormones to pregnant women at risk of preterm birth. Corticosteroid Therapy involves the use of corticosteroids to enhance fetal lung maturity. Tocolytics Therapy aims to delay preterm labor by suppressing uterine contractions. Antihypertensive Therapy helps manage high blood pressure during pregnancy. Magnesium Sulfate Therapy is administered to prevent complications in preterm births. Lastly, there are various other therapies available, which include antibiotics, cerclage, and cervical pessary.</p></p>
