<p><p>The wood splitter market consists of various types of machines designed to split logs into smaller pieces. Electric wood splitters are powered by electricity and are suitable for smaller-scale operations. Hydraulic wood splitters use hydraulic pressure to split wood with higher force, making them ideal for larger logs. PTO-driven wood splitters are powered by a tractor's power take-off shaft. Gasoline engine wood splitters utilize a gasoline engine for mobility and flexibility. Diesel engine wood splitters have similar features as gasoline engine ones but are powered by a diesel engine.</p></p>
