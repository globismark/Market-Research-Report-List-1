<p><p>The residential solar battery market consists of two main types: independent type and integrated type. The independent type refers to a solar battery system that operates separately from the main power grid, allowing homeowners to store excess solar energy produced during the day for use during nighttime or power outages. On the other hand, integrated type systems are connected to the main power grid, enabling homeowners to store surplus solar energy or draw power from the grid during peak demand periods. Both types provide renewable energy solutions for residential properties.</p></p>
