<p><p>The Ambulance Equipment Market consists of various types of equipment used in ambulances. Burn care equipment comprises of burn dressings, pain management supplies, and cooling devices. Blood and hemorrhage control equipment involves tourniquets, hemostatic agents, and pressure dressings. Diagnostic and infection control equipment include blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, and disinfection products. Respiratory, hypothermia, and cardiac equipment consist of oxygen masks, defibrillators, and warming blankets. Other types of equipment may include splints, immobilization devices, and trauma kits.</p></p>
