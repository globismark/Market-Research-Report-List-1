<p><strong>The Digital Insulation Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Voltage</li><li>DC Voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital insulation testers are devices used to measure the resistance and integrity of electrical insulation in different applications. AC voltage insulation testers are designed to test the insulation of electrically powered equipment operating on alternating current. They measure the capacitive current and insulation resistance. On the other hand, DC voltage insulation testers are used to assess insulation resistance in devices operating on direct current. They measure the polarization index and test voltage. Both types of testers are crucial for maintaining electrical safety and preventing equipment failures.</p></p>
