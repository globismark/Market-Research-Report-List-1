Pipettes are handheld devices used for transferring small volumes of liquids. Valves regulate the flow of fluids within the laboratory setup. Wash stations aid in cleaning lab equipment. Tubings are used to transport liquids or gases in the lab. Lastly, there are other unmentioned lab accessories that cater to specific needs of laboratory processes.</p></p>
