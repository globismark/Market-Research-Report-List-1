<p><p>The milking machine market is divided into two types: automatic milking machines and semi-automatic milking machines. Automatic milking machines are fully automated and require no human intervention. They use sensors and robotic technology to locate and attach milking cups to the cow's udders, milk the cow, and then detach the cups. Semi-automatic milking machines, on the other hand, require some human assistance, such as manually attaching and detaching the milking cups. These machines are typically less expensive than automatic machines but still provide some level of automation in the milking process.</p></p>
