<p><p>SSL VPN (Secure Sockets Layer Virtual Private Network) is a type of VPN (Virtual Private Network) technology that uses the SSL/TLS protocol to provide secure remote access to an organization's internal network resources. Unlike traditional VPNs, which require client software to be installed on the user's device, SSL VPN operates through a web browser, making it more convenient and flexible for users.</p><p>The SSL VPN market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for secure remote access solutions, rising adoption of cloud-based services, and the proliferation of mobile devices in the workforce are some of the key factors driving this growth.
