<p><strong>The Microcalorimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Scanning Calorimetry</li><li>Isothermal Microcalorimetry</li><li>Isothermal Titration Calorimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microcalorimeters are devices used to measure small amounts of heat generated or absorbed in a sample. Differential scanning calorimetry (DSC) measures the difference in heat flow between a sample and a reference material as they are heated or cooled. Isothermal microcalorimetry (IMC) measures the heat generated or absorbed by a sample over time at a constant temperature. Isothermal titration calorimetry (ITC) measures the heat released or absorbed during a chemical reaction that occurs when one substance is titrated into another at a constant temperature.</p></p>
