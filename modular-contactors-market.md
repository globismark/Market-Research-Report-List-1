<p><strong>The Modular Contactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Voltage</li><li>Medium Voltage</li><li>Low Voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modular contactors are categorized into three market types based on voltage levels: high voltage, medium voltage, and low voltage. High voltage modular contactors are designed to handle electric currents of over 1,000 volts. They are typically used in industrial applications where large amounts of power are required. Medium voltage contactors are designed for currents ranging from 1,000 to 40,000 volts, commonly used in utility substations and large industrial facilities. Low voltage contactors are designed for currents below 1,000 volts and are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
