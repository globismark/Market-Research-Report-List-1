<p><p>The secured encryption chip market consists of two primary types: contact and contactless. Contact chips require physical contact to establish a connection, usually through a card inserted into a reader. These chips are commonly used in credit cards and identification cards. On the other hand, contactless chips do not require physical contact and can establish a connection through radio frequency communication. These chips are utilized in applications such as contactless payment systems and access control cards. Both types serve the purpose of protecting sensitive information and enhancing security measures.</p></p>
