<p><p>A household water purifier is a device used to remove contaminants from drinking water, making it safe and suitable for consumption. This can be attributed to increasing awareness about waterborne diseases and the need for safe drinking water. <p><p>The household water purifier market consists of several types of purifiers, including RO water purifiers, UV water purifiers, and others. </p><p>RO water purifiers use a reverse osmosis process to remove impurities by passing water through a semi-permeable membrane, effectively removing contaminants. </p><p>UV water purifiers employ ultraviolet rays to kill or inactivate bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms in the water. </p><p>Other water purifiers may include activated carbon filters or combination systems that use multiple purification methods. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences for safe and clean drinking water at home.</p></p>
