<p><p>Copper cable testers are electronic devices used to test and troubleshoot copper cables in various applications such as telecommunications, networking, and electrical installations. They are available in two main types: analog and digital. An analog cable tester uses simple circuitry to detect faults and measure cable continuity, resistance, and capacitance. On the other hand, a digital cable tester utilizes advanced technology to provide more accurate and detailed measurements, and can often include additional features like frequency testing and signal tracing. Both types are important tools for ensuring the proper functioning of copper cables in different industries.</p></p>
