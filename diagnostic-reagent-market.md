<p><ul><li>In Vivo Diagnostic Reagents</li><li>In Vitro Diagnostic Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diagnostics reagent market consists of two main types: in vivo diagnostic reagents and in vitro diagnostic reagents. In vivo diagnostic reagents are used inside the body, such as contrast agents for imaging procedures, while in vitro diagnostic reagents are used outside the body, like blood tests, urine tests, and genetic tests. In vivo reagents help provide visualization of specific areas or organs, aiding in diagnosis, while in vitro reagents assist in detecting and measuring substances in bodily fluids, helping in the diagnosis and monitoring of various diseases and conditions.</p></p>
