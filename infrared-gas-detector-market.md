<p><strong>The Infrared Gas Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point IR Detectors</li><li>Open Path IR Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Gas Detector market consists of two main types: Point IR Detectors and Open Path IR Detectors. Point IR Detectors are compact and stationary devices that detect gas leaks in a specific area. They work by continuously monitoring the surrounding air for changes in infrared radiation caused by gas presence. In contrast, Open Path IR Detectors use a transmitter and receiver to create a detection path across an open area. They are ideal for covering large spaces and offer a wider field of view. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety by detecting hazardous gas leaks.</p></p>
