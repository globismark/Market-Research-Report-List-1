<p><strong>The Capacitive Touchscreen Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Capacitive Pen</li><li>Passive Capacitive Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for capacitive touchscreen pens can be categorized into two types: active capacitive pens and passive capacitive pens. Active capacitive pens use inbuilt power sources, such as batteries, to transmit signals to the touchscreen. This allows for more precise and accurate writing or drawing on the screen. On the other hand, passive capacitive pens do not require any power source and instead rely on the conductivity of the user's hand to interact with the touchscreen. They are generally simpler and more affordable options for touchscreen users.</p></p>
