<p><p>The market for Anisotropic Magneto-Resistive (AMR) sensors can be categorized into three types: 1-axis magnetic sensors, 2-axis magnetic sensors, and 3-axis magnetic sensors. The 1-axis sensors can only measure the magnetic field strength along one direction. The 2-axis sensors are capable of measuring the field strength in two perpendicular directions, while the 3-axis sensors can detect the field strength in all three mutually perpendicular axes. These sensors find applications in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, healthcare, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
