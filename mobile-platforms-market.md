<p><p>1. Tracked Self Propelled Platforms: These mobile platforms are equipped with tracks instead of wheels, enabling them to navigate rough terrains and provide stability for work at height. They are commonly used in construction, mining, and forestry industries.</p><p>2. Rail Road Access Platforms: These platforms are designed specifically for railway maintenance and repair tasks. They have wheels and are able to travel on railroad tracks, providing safe access for workers to perform inspections and repairs on trains and infrastructure.</p><p>3. Telescopic Self Propelled Platforms: These platforms have a telescopic boom that can extend vertically or horizontally, allowing workers to reach high or hard-to-reach areas. They provide flexibility and versatility, commonly used in construction, maintenance, and utility industries.</p><p>4. Articulated Self Propelled Platforms: With multiple hinged sections, articulated platforms offer greater flexibility and maneuverability. They can reach over obstacles or work around obstructions, making them suitable for tasks in narrow or congested spaces, such as building maintenance or film production.</p><p>5. Other types market: This refers to a variety of mobile platforms that may have unique features or serve specific purposes. It can include truck-mounted platforms, scissor lifts, spider lifts, and more.
