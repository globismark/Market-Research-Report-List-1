<p><p>The Global Position System (GPS) Antenna market consists of two types: internal antenna and external antenna. An internal antenna is integrated within the device itself, providing convenience and space-saving benefits. It is commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices. On the other hand, an external antenna is a separate component connected to the device externally. It offers better signal reception because it can be positioned in a more optimal location. External antennas are commonly used in vehicles, ships, and buildings where signal strength may be weaker.</p></p>
