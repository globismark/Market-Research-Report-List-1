<p><p>Self-healing composites are materials that can repair themselves after being damaged. There are two main types of self-healing composites in the market: Intrinsic and Ambush Self-healing Material. Intrinsic self-healing materials contain capsules of a healing agent that is released when the material is damaged, while Ambush self-healing materials have a network of microchannels that can deliver a healing agent to the damaged area. Both types of self-healing composites offer innovative solutions for improving the durability and longevity of various products in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
