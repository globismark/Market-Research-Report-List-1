<p><p>The industrial robotics market comprises various types of robots. Articulated robots have a jointed structure resembling a human arm, offering flexibility and precision. Parallel robots have multiple arms connected to a common base, offering high-speed operations with stability. SCARA robots are ideal for assembly and pick-and-place tasks due to their selective compliance and cylindrical coordinate system. Cylindrical robots have a rotary joint and offer extensive reach in a circular workspace. Cartesian robots have three linear joint axes, enabling precise movements in a rectangular workspace. These robotic types serve different purposes within the industrial sector.</p></p>
