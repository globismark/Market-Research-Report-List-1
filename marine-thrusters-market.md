<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The marine thrusters market includes different types of propulsion systems for ships and boats. Controllable Pitch Propeller (CPP) is a type of marine thruster that allows the pitch angle of the propeller blades to be adjusted while the ship is in operation, enabling better maneuverability and efficiency. On the other hand, Fixed Pitch Propeller (FPP) is a simpler system in which the blade pitch is fixed and cannot be changed during operation. The market includes manufacturers and suppliers offering both CPP and FPP options to cater to the varying needs of the marine industry.</p></p>
