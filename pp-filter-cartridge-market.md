<p><ul><li>PP Melt Blown Filter Cartridges</li><li>PP String Wound Filter Cartridges</li><li>PP Spun Filter Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP Filter Cartridge market includes three prominent types: PP Melt Blown Filter Cartridges, PP String Wound Filter Cartridges, and PP Spun Filter Cartridges. PP Melt Blown Filter Cartridges are made of thermally bonded polypropylene fibers and are effective in removing fine particles. PP String Wound Filter Cartridges consist of tightly wound polypropylene yarns, offering excellent depth filtration for various applications. PP Spun Filter Cartridges are produced using a continuous winding process and offer high dirt-holding capacity and low pressure drop. These types cater to different filtration needs in various industries.</p></p>
