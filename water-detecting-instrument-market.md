<p><p>The water detecting instrument market can be categorized into two types: single parameter instruments and multiparameter instruments. Single parameter instruments only measure one specific characteristic of water, such as pH or temperature. On the other hand, multiparameter instruments can measure multiple water parameters simultaneously, including pH, temperature, conductivity, dissolved oxygen, and turbidity. These instruments provide a comprehensive analysis of water quality. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, such as environmental monitoring, research, and water treatment, ensuring accurate and efficient water analysis.</p></p>
