<p><p>The band saw blades market is segmented into three types: bimetal band saw blades, carbide tipped band saw blades, and carbon band saw blades. Bimetal band saw blades are composed of two different metals, offering enhanced durability and cutting performance. Carbide tipped band saw blades have carbide tips on the teeth, making them suitable for cutting hard materials like steel and titanium. Carbon band saw blades are made from high carbon steel and are commonly used for general cutting applications. These different types cater to varying cutting needs and materials in the market.</p></p>
