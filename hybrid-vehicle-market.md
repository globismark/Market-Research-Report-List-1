<p><strong>The Hybrid Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Vehicle</li><li>Series Hybrid Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parallel hybrid vehicles have both a gasoline engine and an electric motor that work together to propel the vehicle. The gasoline engine can either drive the vehicle directly or charge the battery that powers the electric motor. In series hybrid vehicles, only the electric motor drives the vehicle, while the gasoline engine acts as a generator to charge the battery. Both types of hybrid vehicles offer improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions compared to traditional gasoline-powered vehicles.</p></p>
