<p><ul><li>Disposable</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical diagnostic electrode market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use and are discarded after each patient. They are cost-effective, minimize cross-contamination risks, and ensure hygiene. Reusable electrodes, on the other hand, can be used multiple times after undergoing proper sterilization processes. They are more durable and environmentally friendly but come with a higher initial cost. Both types play a crucial role in accurately measuring electrical activity in various medical procedures, enabling efficient diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
