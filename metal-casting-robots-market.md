<p><p>Metal casting robots refer to the use of robotic systems in the manufacturing process of casting metal parts. There are two main types of robots used in this market. Semi-automatic robots require some human intervention and monitoring during the casting process. They perform specific tasks such as pouring or removing molds. On the other hand, fully automatic robots are capable of independently carrying out the entire casting process, from pouring the molten metal to removing the finished parts. These robots are programmed to operate without human involvement, increasing productivity and efficiency in the metal casting industry.</p></p>
