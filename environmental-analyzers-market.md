<p><p>Environmental analyzers are devices that are used to measure and monitor various environmental parameters such as air quality, water quality, and soil composition. There are two main types of environmental analyzers: portable and benchtop. Portable analyzers are compact and lightweight, allowing for field measurements and on-site analysis. They are ideal for quick and easy testing in various environmental conditions. On the other hand, benchtop analyzers are larger and more powerful, designed for laboratory settings where more detailed and precise measurements are required. They offer higher sensitivity and accuracy but are not easily portable.</p></p>
