<p><p>Insulated panels, also known as sandwich panels, are composite building materials that consist of two layers of rigid board materials or facings with a core insulation material in between. <p><strong>The Insulated Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPS Panels</li><li>PUR/PIR Panels</li><li>Mineral/Glass Wool Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulated panels are widely used in the construction industry for their energy-efficient properties. EPS panels, also known as expanded polystyrene panels, consist of foam insulation sandwiched between two sheets of material. PUR/PIR panels utilize polyurethane or polyisocyanurate insulation, offering superior thermal performance. Mineral/glass wool panels, on the other hand, are made from natural or synthetic fibers and provide excellent fire resistance. These three types of insulated panels cater to different construction requirements, providing varying degrees of insulation, fire resistance, and thermal performance.</p></p>
