<p><p>Film screen mammography and digital mammography are two types of breast cancer screening equipment used in medical imaging. Film screen mammography uses X-ray film and intensifying screens to produce images of the breast tissue. Digital mammography, on the other hand, uses digital technology to capture and display the images directly on a computer, eliminating the need for film. Digital mammography offers advantages such as quicker image acquisition, better image quality, and the ability to digitally enhance and manipulate the images for a more accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
