<p><p>Blood glucose strips and meters are devices used to monitor and measure the level of glucose in the blood. They are commonly used by people with diabetes to manage their condition. The meter then displays the glucose level on a screen, allowing the user to track and manage their blood sugar levels.</p><p>The blood glucose strips and meter market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, mainly driven by the increasing prevalence of diabetes globally. The rising diabetic population, coupled with the growing awareness of the importance of diabetes management, has led to an increased demand for blood glucose strips and meters.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in blood glucose monitoring devices have also contributed to market growth. The introduction of wireless and smartphone-compatible blood glucose meters has made monitoring glucose levels more convenient for users.
