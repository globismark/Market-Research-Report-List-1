<p><strong>The Fully Automated Liquid Handling Workstation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Liquid Handling Workstation</li><li>Contact Type Liquid Handling Workstation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully automated liquid handling workstation market consists of two major types: non-contact liquid handling workstations and contact type liquid handling workstations. Non-contact liquid handling workstations use technologies like acoustic droplet ejection and air displacement to transfer small volumes of liquids without physically contacting the samples. Contact type liquid handling workstations, on the other hand, use pipettes or syringes to directly touch the samples and transfer the liquids. Both types offer precise and accurate liquid handling capabilities for various applications in research laboratories, pharmaceutical companies, and biotechnology industries.</p></p>
