The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, an increasing number of individuals are adopting plant-based diets due to growing health concerns and environmental consciousness. With a growing number of individuals suffering from these conditions, soy milk serves as a viable alternative that meets their dietary requirements.</p><p>Additionally, the market has witnessed the introduction of innovative flavors and variants of soy milk, appealing to a wider consumer base. <p><p>The soy milk market is broadly categorized into three types: soy milk powder, normal temperature soy milk, and refrigerated soy milk. Soy milk powder is made by removing the water content from soy milk, which is then converted into powder form for ease of use and longer shelf life. Normal temperature soy milk is packaged and sold directly without any special storage requirements. Refrigerated soy milk, on the other hand, needs to be stored in a cold environment to maintain its freshness. These different market types cater to various consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
