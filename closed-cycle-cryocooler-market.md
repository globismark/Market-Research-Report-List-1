<p><p>A closed cycle cryocooler is a type of cryocooler that operates on a closed cycle refrigeration process. It uses a compressed gas, often helium or nitrogen, to cool down a heat sink which in turn cools down the target object. This process is repeated in a closed loop, hence the name "closed cycle."</p><p>The closed cycle cryocooler market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key driving factors for market growth is the increasing demand for cryogenics in various applications such as aerospace, defense, healthcare, and electronics.
