<p><p>Blood thaw equipment refers to devices designed to thaw frozen blood products effectively and efficiently. There are two types of market segments in this industry: manual blood thawing and automated blood thawing. Manual blood thawing involves the use of handheld equipment or water baths, which requires human intervention for monitoring and regulating the thawing process. On the other hand, automated blood thawing utilizes advanced technology such as dry thawing systems or microwave-assisted thawing to automate the process and reduce human involvement. Both types aim to ensure safe and precise thawing of blood products, but automated equipment offers enhanced convenience and accuracy.</p></p>
