<p><strong>The Aluminum Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum castings market comprises two major types: die casting and permanent mold casting. Die casting involves injecting molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, resulting in complex shapes with fine details. It is widely used in automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics industries. Permanent mold casting, on the other hand, uses reusable molds and gravity to fill them with molten aluminum. This method is preferred for producing larger and simpler shapes, such as engine blocks and pump housings, due to its lower cost and longer tool life.</p></p>
