<p><p>Semiconductor Assembly and Test Equipment market consists of various types of equipment used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. Wafer Probe Station is used for electrically testing semiconductor devices on a wafer, while Die Bonder is used to attach small semiconductor chips onto a substrate. Dicing Machine is employed to separate individual chips from a semiconductor wafer. Test handler is responsible for handling and testing packaged semiconductor devices, while Sorter helps in sorting and categorizing them. Lastly, "Others" category includes various equipment such as wire bonders and encapsulation machines used in the assembly and testing process.</p></p>
