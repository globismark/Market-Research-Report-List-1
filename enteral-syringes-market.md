The growing geriatric population, increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, and rising demand for home healthcare services are some of the key factors driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of innovative enteral syringes with features such as safety caps, anti-clogging systems, and accurate dose measurements are further contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also played a role in boosting the demand for enteral syringes. The need for safe and efficient delivery of nutritional supplements and medications to patients, especially those in critical condition, has increased the adoption of enteral feeding devices, including enteral syringes.</p><p>Some of the latest trends observed in the enteral syringes market include the introduction of syringes with integrated feeding pumps, which offer ease of use and precise medication delivery. Additionally, the growing preference for enteral nutrition over parenteral nutrition is expected to drive the demand for enteral syringes in the coming years.</p><p>Overall, the enteral syringes market is projected to experience steady growth, with a CAGR of 8.10% during the forecast period.
