<p><p>The large format printer market consists of two main types: inkjet printers and laser printers. Inkjet printers utilize tiny droplets of ink that are sprayed onto the printing media to form the desired image or text. They are known for producing high-quality prints with vibrant colors. On the other hand, laser printers use a laser beam to transfer toner onto the printing media. They are renowned for their speed and accuracy, making them suitable for large-scale printing tasks. Both inkjet and laser printers cater to the needs of businesses and professionals requiring oversized prints.</p></p>
