<p><p>The Cervical Cancer Diagnostic Tests market consists of various types of tests used to detect cervical cancer. Pap Smear Tests involve collecting cells from the cervix to examine them for any abnormalities. Colposcopy is a procedure that uses a special microscope to examine the cervix for abnormal tissue. HPV Testing detects the presence of human papillomavirus, a common cause of cervical cancer. Endocervical Curettage involves scraping cells from the cervix's canal for further examination. Cone Biopsy is a surgical procedure that removes a cone-shaped piece of tissue for analysis. Other tests may include molecular tests or imaging techniques.</p></p>
