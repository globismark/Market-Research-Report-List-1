<p><p>The Ultrasonic Linear Probe market consists of three main types: the Ultrasound Straight Probe, Ultrasound Angle Probe, and Ultrasound Curvature Probe. The Ultrasound Straight Probe is designed with a straight beam path that allows for imaging at a fixed depth. The Ultrasound Angle Probe, on the other hand, features an angled beam path that enables imaging from different angles. Lastly, the Ultrasound Curvature Probe is engineered with a curved design, allowing for better visualization and imaging of curved structures in the body.</p></p>
