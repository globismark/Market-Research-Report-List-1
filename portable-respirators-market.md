<p><p>Portable respirators are essential devices used to provide respiratory support to individuals with breathing difficulties. Constant pressure type respirators maintain a consistent pressure throughout the breathing cycle, allowing ease of breath. Fixed volume type respirators deliver a fixed volume of air or oxygen with each breath. Timing type respirators provide breathing assistance based on a specific time interval. Mixed-type respirators combine features of constant pressure, fixed volume, and timing respirators, providing a customized approach to patient needs. These market types reflect the various options available to cater to different respiratory requirements.</p></p>
