<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The post-reflow machine market consists of different types of machines used in the electronics manufacturing industry. Convection ovens are one type, which use hot air circulation to evenly heat the circuit boards. They are popular due to their quick heating and cooling capabilities. Another type is the vapor phase oven, which uses a liquid with a low boiling point to transfer heat to the circuit boards. This method provides excellent soldering results and is beneficial for sensitive components. Both types are essential for achieving reliable and efficient soldering in electronics production.</p></p>
