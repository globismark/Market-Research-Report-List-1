<p><ul><li>Wall Mounted</li><li>Drop-in</li><li>Undermount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bath sinks market boasts three main types: wall mounted, drop-in, and undermount. Wall mounted sinks are affixed directly to the wall, providing a sleek and space-saving solution. Drop-in sinks, also known as self-rimming or top-mount sinks, sit on top of the countertop, leaving a visible rim. Undermount sinks are installed beneath the counter for a seamless and contemporary look. Each type has its own advantages and aesthetics, allowing consumers to choose according to their preference and bathroom design.</p></p>
