<p><p>RF and Microwave filters are electronic devices used to remove unwanted frequencies from signals in the RF and Microwave frequency ranges. There are different types of filters available, including Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters use acoustic waves on a piezoelectric substrate to filter the signals, while BAW filters use bulk waves within a resonator structure. Both SAW and BAW filters are commonly used in wireless communication systems and have specific advantages and applications depending on the desired frequency range and performance requirements.</p></p>
