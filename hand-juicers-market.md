To extract the juice, the user simply places a cut fruit or vegetable on the container and presses down while rotating the handle.</p><p>The hand juicers market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. <p><p>The Hand Juicers market includes two popular types: Vertical Auger Model and Horizontal Auger Model. The Vertical Auger Model operates by crushing fruits and vegetables against a rotating vertical auger, extracting the juice from them. It is known to produce higher juice yields and requires less effort compared to other models. On the other hand, the Horizontal Auger Model operates with a horizontal auger and uses a slower rotation.
