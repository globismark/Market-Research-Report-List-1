<p><p>The baby movement monitor market includes three types: under-the-mattress, diaper attachment, and smart wearable devices. Under-the-mattress monitors are placed beneath the baby's mattress, detecting movements and transmitting alerts to parents. Diaper attachment monitors are clipped to the baby's diaper, monitoring movements and sending notifications when changes occur. Lastly, smart wearable monitors are designed as wearable devices for babies, tracking their movements and providing real-time updates to parents. These different types of monitors cater to the varying needs and preferences of parents in ensuring their baby's safety and well-being.</p></p>
