<p><p>Tuned passive harmonic filters are used to mitigate harmonic distortions in electrical systems. There are three main market types: single tuned filters, double tuned filters, and triple tuned filters. Single tuned filters are designed to attenuate a specific harmonic frequency, while double tuned filters address two specific harmonic frequencies. Triple tuned filters are used to mitigate three specific harmonic frequencies. Each type of filter is tailored to target and reduce the specific harmonic distortions present in a particular electrical system.</p></p>
