<p><p>Freeze-dried foods are a type of processed food that undergoes a unique preservation method called freeze-drying. This involves freezing the food and then subjecting it to a vacuum, which removes the ice through sublimation, leaving behind the dehydrated food. Freeze-drying helps to retain the nutritional value, taste, texture, and aroma of the food for an extended period. It also makes the food lightweight and easy to rehydrate, making it a popular choice for camping, emergency food supplies, and space missions.</p><p>The freeze-dried foods market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The rising demand for convenient and healthy food products, along with the increasing popularity of outdoor activities and adventure sports, is driving the market. Moreover, the growing awareness regarding the benefits of freeze-dried foods, such as its long shelf life, nutrient retention, and ease of storage, is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has also significantly impacted the freeze-dried foods market. As people are spending more time at home and avoiding restaurants, they are opting for freeze-dried foods as a convenient and safe alternative.
