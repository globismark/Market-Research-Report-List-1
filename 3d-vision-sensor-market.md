<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for 3D vision sensors can be categorized into two types: stereo vision and structured light. Stereo vision utilizes two cameras to capture images from slightly different angles, mimicking human binocular vision. This enables accurate depth perception and object recognition. On the other hand, structured light involves projecting a pattern of light onto objects and analyzing the deformation of the pattern to calculate depth information. Both technologies are widely adopted in a range of industries such as robotics, automotive, and healthcare for various applications requiring precise 3D imaging and spatial understanding.</p></p>
