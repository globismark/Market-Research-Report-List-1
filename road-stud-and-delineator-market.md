<p><p>Road stud and delineator are both transportation safety devices used to improve visibility and guide drivers on the road.</p><p>Road studs, also known as cat's eyes or raised pavement markers, are small reflectors embedded in the road surface. They use retro-reflective materials to reflect light from vehicle headlights back to the driver, enhancing visibility during nighttime or adverse weather conditions. Road studs come in various types, including traditional non-reflective studs, reflective studs, solar-powered studs, and intelligent studs equipped with features like LED lights.</p><p>Delineators, on the other hand, are upright markers placed at the edge of roads or in the median to indicate lane boundaries, curves, or hazards. They effectively guide drivers, especially during low-visibility conditions. Delineators are available in different forms such as flexible posts, reflective panels, and tubular markers.</p><p>The Road Stud and Delineator Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing focus on road safety by governments and transportation authorities is one of the major factors driving the market growth. The rising number of road accidents and fatalities worldwide has led to the implementation of stringent road safety regulations, including the installation of road studs and delineators.</p><p>Additionally, the expansion of road infrastructure, especially in developing economies, is boosting the demand for road studs and delineators.
