<p><p>Surgical glue, also known as tissue adhesive or surgical adhesive, is a medical adhesive used to close wounds or incisions during surgery or other medical procedures. It is a type of liquid or gel substance that adheres to the skin, holding the edges of the wound together and promoting healing. Surgical glue eliminates the need for traditional stitches or sutures, offering benefits such as reduced scarring, decreased risk of infection, and faster healing time.</p><p>The global surgical glue market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The market growth is primarily driven by factors such as the increasing number of surgical procedures, advancements in surgical glue technology, and rising preference for minimally invasive surgeries. One such trend is the development of bio-based surgical glue. Manufacturers are focusing on developing surgical adhesives using biological materials that are safe and biocompatible. These bio-based adhesives offer advantages such as reduced toxicity, improved wound healing, and minimal tissue damage.</p><p>Another trend in the surgical glue market is the increased adoption of surgical glue in cosmetic surgeries.
