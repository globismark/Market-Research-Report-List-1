<p><p>The horizontal single stage centrifugal pump market consists of two main types: self-priming centrifugal pumps and non-self-priming centrifugal pumps. Self-priming centrifugal pumps are designed to automatically remove air and can operate without the need for manual priming. These pumps are commonly used in applications where air or vapor may be present in the fluid. On the other hand, non-self-priming centrifugal pumps require manual priming before operation and are widely used in various industries for moving liquids.</p></p>
