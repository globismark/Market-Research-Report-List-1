<p><p>Cognitive screening tools are used to assess an individual's cognitive abilities and detect any potential cognitive impairment. The market for these tools includes various types such as Mini-Mental State Exam (MMSE), Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA), and Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR). The MMSE evaluates memory, attention, and language skills, while the MoCA assesses multiple cognitive domains. The CDR is specifically designed to classify dementia severity. These tools aid healthcare professionals in diagnosing and monitoring cognitive disorders, providing valuable insights for appropriate treatment and patient care.</p></p>
