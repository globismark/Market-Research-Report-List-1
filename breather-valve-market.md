<p><p>A breather valve, also known as a pressure/vacuum relief valve, is a safety device used in various industries to regulate and control the pressure and vacuum levels in equipment such as tanks, vessels, and pipelines. It allows the exchange of gases between the equipment and the atmosphere, preventing excessive pressure buildup or vacuum conditions that could potentially damage the equipment or cause safety hazards.</p><p>The breather valve market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of safety measures in various industrial sectors, such as oil and gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, and manufacturing. The market is driven by the growing awareness about the importance of maintaining optimum pressure levels in equipment to ensure safe operations and prevent accidents.</p><p>Furthermore, stringent regulations imposed by government authorities to promote workplace safety and environmental protection are contributing to the market growth.
